Omori follows

Sunny

A hikikomori boy living in Faraway town who has suffered some severe psychological trauma. The term hikikomori is a Japanese term which refers to those who lock themselves away from the outside world for years.

Omori

Sunny's dream world avatar, and the player character during the dream portions of the game.



You have been living here for as long as you can remember. The game's introduction to Sunny's dream world, White Space is an empty and cold void. One of the later characters in the game will tell you that to be in white space is to be nothing. White Space is emptiness, a home without warmth. A place to survive, but not to live. This sterile void is where Sunny, as Omori, escapes from anything that may bring back memories of his trauma in his subconscious. Omori's laptop reveals that he has spent an eternity here, every entry concluding: "Everything was okay.". This journal also tell us that when he isn't locking himself away in White Space, he's visiting his friends. Headspace

Mari

Sunny's sister, the mom-type friend, and Hero's girlfriend. She often provides advice and food for the group as they adventure, acting as a save and healing point throughout the game.

Basil

The shy, but thoughtful gardener and photographer. Sunny's best friend.

Kel

The comedic and competitive good natured knucklehead. He often gets into fights with Aubrey as they tease each other.

Aubrey

The enthusiastic and empathetic tomboy. She often fights with Kel as they tease each other.

Hero

The big brother figure. He often keeps the peace between Aubrey and Kel and makes them make up after their fights.




But the real world segments of the game aren't so cheerful. Mari is dead. Sunny hasn't left his house in four years. And his friends, the same ones we met in Headspace, are broken versions of their former selves, still reeling from Mari's suicide. The game begins with three days remaining until Sunny moves away from Faraway town.

Real World

Kel

He reaches out to Sunny in order to talk with him and hang out before Sunny moves away. He often stands up for others, including his old friends, and tries to help out.

Aubrey

She often acts out, and is the leader of her new group of friends, which acts more like a small town gang of bullies.

Hero

Hero went off to college, and only returns later in the game. He acts much the same, attempting to maintain peace among his former friends and tries to get everyone to talk to each other and hears out everyone's side fairly.




In the beginning sequences of the game in Headspace, you'll help Basil put back together his photo album, which got disorganized after an quarrel between Aubrey and Kel. But once you've finished, Basil will notice another photo on the ground. Picking it up causes a strange shadow to gather around him, and his fear-filled eyes turn red, as he yells out Mari's name.

But the next time you visit Headspace, Basil is nowhere to be found. The group elects to search for him. However, as you continue, your friends begin to forget what Basil looks like and what he likes to do, until he is entirely erased from their memories, as you get caught up in other conquests. But no bandage can stifle an eternal wound, and there will be a time when its influence will bleed through. Basil's shadowy apparition always appears that the conclusion of Sunny's dreams, reminding him of a piece of the harrowing reality he represses.
